Privacy Policy

Welcome to Parallel Space (the "App"). We understand the importance of your privacy and fully respect and protect your data security. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain how we collect, use, store, and disclose your personal information, as well as the rights you have to manage your data. This policy fully complies with the User Data and Security requirements of the Google Play Developer Policies.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the core functionality of app cloning/multi-accounting in a sandbox environment, we may only collect and process the minimum information necessary to maintain the app's operation:

  • Device & Diagnostic Information: Includes device model, OS version, crash logs, and performance data. These data are typically anonymized and used exclusively to improve product stability and user experience.
  • Installed Apps List: We need to read the list of installed applications on your device (using the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission) so you can select which apps to clone in the interface. This list is processed locally and is NEVER uploaded to our servers.

2. App Permissions Explanation

To ensure that cloned third-party applications run normally within their internal environment, the App itself may request the following permissions from you. Please note: we are not collecting your data through these permissions; we are simply granting the cloned apps the system access they need to function properly:

  • Storage Permission: Used to allocate storage space to save independent data, images, and files for the cloned applications.
  • Camera / Microphone Permission: Requested by the system only when you need to use the camera or voice features within a cloned app (e.g., WhatsApp, WeChat).
  • Location Permission: Called only when a cloned third-party application requires location services while in use.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure (Third-Party SDKs)

We promise not to sell or rent your personal information. The App integrates some compliant third-party SDKs (to provide advertising and analytical services), which may collect Advertising Identifiers (Google Ad ID) and certain device information:

  • Google Play Services / AdMob: Used for in-app advertising and basic services. For specific data processing rules, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics: Used for statistical analysis and error log reporting to improve app stability.

4. Data Retention and Your Rights (Data Deletion)

Local Data Storage: The vast majority of your app data is securely encrypted and stored locally in the cloned space on your device. We do NOT upload the sensitive communication content or user data of the cloned third-party applications to our servers.

Right to Data Deletion: In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety policy, you have the right to delete your data at any time.

  • Local Data: You can delete all local cloned space data and archives at any time by going to your device's "Settings" -> "Apps" -> clearing the App's data ("Clear Data"), or by simply uninstalling the App completely.
  • Other Requests: If you have any specific data records that you wish we delete from our cloud-related logs, please feel free to contact us via the email provided at the bottom of this policy.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures (such as encryption technology during transmission) to protect your information and prevent unauthorized access, public disclosure, or destruction of related data.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable legal age limit in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, we will permanently delete such data as quickly as possible.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make material changes to the policy, we will notify you prominently within the App. Your continued use of the App following any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
